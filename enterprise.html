ZML Web Player

The ZML web player was designed specifically for the corporate retail environment with a low bitrate to accommodate the IT needs of companies. Music is streamed over the internet to the local computer in the store location and connected directly to the audio system.

ZML Streaming Player

The ZML dedicated Streaming Music Player is the product we suggest when you want hands free music streamed to your locations. The ZML Music Player plays music without a computer. It's built for 24/7 commercial usage and has a small footprint.
